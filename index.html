About Us

Kaizen Logistics Pte Ltd was established as a limited company in 2015 with its humble beginnings since 2013. From a simple courier service operation, we are proud to have expanded in both our physical ground fleet, and our footprint in the logistics sector. Learn More

Mission

To achieve lean supply chain logistics while enabling efficiency through agile processes and technology advancements. Eliminate waste and focus on client fulfilment.

Vision

To be ASEAN’s preferred logistics enabler across countries through technology, innovation and operational process.

Our Specialisation

Same Day Delivery

At Kaizen, we deploy our unique "1-day delivery" rule and hold zero inventory, at the end of every business day. Our efficient and automated processes convert inbound inventory into transit, at the end of each day.

Warehousing Facilities

We provide state of the art and highly advanced warehouse facilities with 24/7 CCTV surveillance. Our warehouses present ambient storage facilities to our clients, and ensure 100% customer satisfaction through our outstanding AVA certified infrastructure in Singapore.

24/7 Support

We have implemented single-SPOC client framework to ensure undivided attention and resolution of each and every query of our clients at any time of the day. Our 24/7 customer assistance team of highly trained and effective operatives, will make sure that your voice, never goes unheard.

Secured applications – 2FTP

Our in-house last mile delivery application is built on the latest technology, and ensure secure and encrypted data transfer, to provide 2 step verification security level protection for our every transaction. Our dedicated app helps in executing reliable same day delivery through features like digital signature, OTP verification, and automated notifications.

Insurance. $1 Million

Our SLA certified logistics services add unmatched powers and capabilities to our client's supply chain. We proudly offer guarantee up to $1 million to each of our client's cargo, while it is under our possession.

Asia Centric yet Global

Situated in Singapore, we have a highly extensive regional delivery network and deep on-ground knowledge of the logistics paradigm across various countries in Asia. We manage a highly distributed network of efficient and specialized strategic partners, across the globe, helping us to deliver our unparalleled services to each and every doorstep across all geographies.

Urgent/AOG Shipments

Kaizen Logistics is the master of urgent deliveries, with our expert delivery team, containing highly dedicated AOG professionals and highly specialized and automated procedures, we assure urgent deliveries of critical cargo across the globe, through the fastest routes and in a minimum time frame.

Affordable & Cost effective

Through technology, we are able to reduce down your logistics costs to a minimum while ensuring quality and standards

Transparent & Paperless

We have built a highly advanced Kaizen portal, a software application built on the latest technology, to accelerate the delivery process across all geographies, and at the same time transforming all our internal operations paperless, through features like e-invoicing and unified digital communication channels.
